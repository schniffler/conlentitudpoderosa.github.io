5 Important Facts About Cells

  • Every living thing is made up of cells
  • Cells are microscopic, meaning they can't be seen with the naked human eye
  • Animal and plant cells are different
  • All living things are made of cells, BUT, only living things or things that were at one point living have cells
  • Cells are the basic building blocks, but cells make up tissues, which make up organs, which make up the human body
